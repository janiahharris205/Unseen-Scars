What is PTSD?

Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) is a psychiatric disorder most commonly associated with veterans who experience PTSD symptoms after war. However, anyone who experiences or witnesses a traumatic event could possibly suffer from PTSD. Some events other than war that can cause PTSD are neglect, sexual assault, domestic violence, bullying, natural disasters, and serious accedents.

PTSD symptoms are categorized into four types: intrusive symptoms, avoidance symptoms, negative thoughts and/or moods, and hyperreactivity.
